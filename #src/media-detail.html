Jim Adler TV Spots Broadcast on Last Week Tonight with John Oliver

By Jim Adler, May 24, 2019

A popular late-night show on HBO, Last Week Tonight with John Oliver, featured a video collage of The Texas Hammer’s famous spots in both English and Spanish. The cable show features satirical recaps of a variety of both World and U.S. news headlines and airs on a weekly basis.

The May 5th show paired its news discussion with a short comedic segment titled, “And Now This” where producers merged together a variety of Jim Adler’s most popular TV spots and cut them into the show. See the English and Spanish video collages here.
